<h1>Youth Allowance</h1>
<p style="text-align: left; padding-left: 40px;">Youth Allowance is an income support payment that provides financial assistance to young job seekers and students.</p>
<p style="text-align: left; padding-left: 40px;">Scroll down and select the tile according to your profile.&nbsp;Find how much you can claim from Youth Allowance.&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left; padding-left: 40px;">(*You must be an Australian Citizen living in Australia to claim it).</p>
